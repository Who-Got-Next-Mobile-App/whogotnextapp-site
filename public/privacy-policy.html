Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Effective Date: January 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Who Got Next™ ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our App, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, profile photo (optional), and bio
  • Game Data: Game statistics, win/loss records, rankings, and game history
  • Social Information: Friends list, friend requests, blocked users, and messages

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, screens viewed, time spent in app, interactions with content
  • Location Data: Precise location (GPS coordinates) when you use location-based features
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, performance metrics, error logs

1.3 Health & Fitness Data (Optional)

If you enable wearable device integration, we may collect:

  • Heart rate data during games
  • Calories burned
  • Steps and distance covered
  • Active minutes
  • Movement intensity data

Note: Health data collection requires your explicit consent and can be disabled at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Direct Collection: Information you provide when creating an account, updating your profile, or using app features
  • Automatic Collection: Data collected automatically through app usage, device sensors, and analytics tools
  • Third-Party Services: Data collected through integrated services like Firebase (authentication and database), Google Maps (location services), and analytics providers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Connect you with other players and facilitate game organization
  • Display nearby courts and games based on your location
  • Calculate and display rankings, statistics, and achievements
  • Send push notifications about game invites, messages, and friend requests
  • Prevent fraud, verify game participation, and ensure fair play
  • Track fitness performance and provide health insights (if enabled)
  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our App:

  • Firebase (Google): Backend infrastructure, user authentication, cloud database storage, and analytics
  • Google Maps/MapKit: Location services and map display
  • Analytics Providers: App usage analytics and crash reporting to improve app performance
  • Cloud Storage Providers: Secure storage of user-generated content (profile photos, etc.)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Public Profile Information

Your profile information (display name, photo, game statistics, and rank) is visible to other App users to facilitate game organization and social features. You can control some visibility settings in your profile.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Who Got Next, our users, or others
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion
  • Game History: Retained permanently for statistics and rankings (unless you request deletion)
  • Messages: Retained until you delete them or your account is deleted
  • Location Snapshots: Retained for 90 days for fraud prevention purposes
  • Health Data: Retained until you delete it or disable wearable integration
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form

Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for messages
  • Secure Firebase authentication (industry-standard authentication protocols)
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure provided by Google Cloud Platform

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Export: Request an export of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications, location sharing, or wearable data collection
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Object to Processing: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@whogotnext.app or use the in-app settings (Settings → Data & Privacy).

8. Location Data

We collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) to:

  • Show nearby courts on the map
  • Connect you with local players
  • Display games in your area
  • Verify game participation for fraud prevention

Location Access: Location data is collected only when you grant permission and use location-based features. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings, though this will limit app functionality.

Background Location: We do not collect location data when the app is running in the background, except during active games for verification purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@whogotnext.app. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

We comply with applicable data protection regulations:

  • GDPR (EU Users): We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation for users in the European Union
  • CCPA (California Residents): We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Other Jurisdictions: We respect local data protection laws where applicable

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our App uses analytics tools that may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect usage information. These tools help us understand how users interact with our App. You can control tracking through your device settings.

We do not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes or to track you across other apps or websites.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Sending an email notification (if you have provided an email address)
  • Displaying an in-app notification

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Third-Party Links

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as Spotify, YouTube) that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.
